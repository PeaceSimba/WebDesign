*SATURDAY NIGHT*

ACTIVITY;
  1. Adult Associate Classes-These non-syllabus masterclasses focus on ballet technique, music and artistry, under the guidance of experienced LBDC faculty. Classes are designed to enhance core-strength and fitness
  2. Intermediate foundation Classes-Designed for intensive study of the syllabus in preparation for exams, classes take place over 5 consecutive Saturdays and include a pointe work class for females and a virtuosity class for males
  3. Cinderella Children's Repertoire Workshop-Discover the world of classical ballet through dance, music, acting and storytelling. Each workshop will take a different scene and characters from this magical tale and closes with a presentation for parents and guardians.
