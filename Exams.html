Around the world, LBDC exams are recognised for their rigour and quality. Each year, nearly a quarter of a million candidates across the globe choose to take their dance exams with us.


We offer dance exams for members twice per year through the International Dance Teachers’ Association (IDTA) and reward students of all ages and abilities. Our focus is on creativity and musicality, providing opportunities to progress and achieve. It’s not just professional or young dancers who take our exams; age is no barrier to success. At any stage of your life or career you can develop your fitness, musicality and artistry. Our candidates range in age from 2 to 60+!
